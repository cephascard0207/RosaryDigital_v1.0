Commerce

Dive deep into subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies,Economics, Languages to build a strong foundation in financial literacy, management principles, and market dynamics. Our courses are designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed for careers in business, finance, and accountancy.

Science

From Physics and Chemistry to Biology and Mathematics, our comprehensive curriculum offers a rigorous understanding of scientific principles and their real-world applications. Prepare yourself for a future in medicine, engineering, research, and beyond with our cutting-edge resources and interactive learning tools.

Arts

Unleash your creativity and critical thinking with our Arts stream. Study subjects like History, Sociology, Political Science, and Psychology to gain insights into human behavior, society, and culture. This stream is perfect for those who aspire to careers in law, journalism, education, social work, or any field where understanding human dynamics is key.

Computer Technique

Step into the future with our Computer Technique stream. Learn the fundamentals of programming, web development, database management, and more. Our hands-on approach ensures you gain practical skills in modern technologies, preparing you for a successful career in IT, software development, and digital innovation.

Catering & Restaurant Management

Satisfy your passion for culinary arts and hospitality with our Catering and Restaurant Management stream. Learn everything from food preparation and service management to customer relations and business operations. This stream is your gateway to a dynamic career in the food and beverage industry, with opportunities in hotels, restaurants, and event management.

Health Care Services

Make a difference in people's lives with our Healthcare Services stream. Study subjects like Anatomy, Physiology, Healthcare Management, and Nursing to gain the knowledge and skills needed to pursue a career in the medical field. Whether you aim to be a nurse, medical assistant, or healthcare administrator, our courses provide a strong foundation for your future in healthcare.
